recent Victories



Humans for Humans served its first school meals in Turkana primary schools last month, reaching more than 60, 000 children in 171 primary schools with daily meals of maize and beans with fortified oil. The magnitude of serving school meals here is summed up in the phrase ‘no smoke, no school’; if there is no activity in school kitchens, children will not attend lessons. Many children are wholly reliant on school feeding to survive as the region is currently enduring severe drought after four consecutive failed rainy seasons resulting in crop failure and low yield harvests
